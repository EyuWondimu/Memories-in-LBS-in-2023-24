Lich Gogo Exellency Boardig School

This is one of the well known boarding school in Ethiopia

Objective

To creat students who can influence their envirnment and country in positive aspects like

  • Mathematics,
  • Physics,
  • Leadership ...

Education Process

  • Given by skilled teachers,
    • The teacher must have at least MS and above.
  • Teaches sellected students from zone by exam,
  • Curriculum based and lab supported education,
  • From Monday up to Friday each week,
  • At least 45 students in one class.

Administration

It is adminstrated by Hadiya Development Association(HDA); head office in Hossana.

Skills necessary to apply

  • Having a good score in elementery classes,
  • Scoring more than 75 average in ministry exam,
  • Being able to pass the entrance exam given by school.

application Time

Specially from September to October but it depends on the situation
